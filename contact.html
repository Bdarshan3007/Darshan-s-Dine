❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified within 5 minutes of placement. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

What are your delivery charges?

Delivery is free for orders above ₹500 within 5 km. Standard delivery charge applies otherwise.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We offer customized catering for events and parties. Call us for details and quotes.

What are your payment options?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on delivery. Split payments available too!

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We have an extensive vegetarian menu with over 20 delicious options.

How do I book a table?

You can book tables through our website, call us directly, or visit us in person. Group reservations welcome!
